August 7, 2015
Henry Doorly Zoo & Aquarium – Omaha, NE USA

Details

  • Single day, single track conference dedicated to Web Development and JavaScript.
  • After party features special musical guest, The Superbytes.
  • Held at the Omaha Zoo, named the #1 Zoo in the World by TripAdvisor.
  • Conference passes include Zoo admission, a free NEJS Conference T-shirt, and Lunch (and Snacks).
  • Brought to you by NebraskaJS (Omaha) and organized by the NEJS CONF Team.

The centerpiece of the opposite wall is a 24 foot 10,000 gallon reef aquarium.” —via omahazoo.com
