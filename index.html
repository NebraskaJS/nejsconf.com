August 7, 2015
Henry Doorly Zoo & Aquarium – Omaha, NE USA

Details

  • Single day, single track conference dedicated to Web Development and JavaScript.
  • Held at the Omaha Henry Doorly Zoo, named the #1 Zoo in the World by TripAdvisor.
  • Conference passes include:
    • Transportation to and from the Hotel and the Zoo.
    • Free Zoo Admission for the day
    • Tasty beverages from the Aromas Coffee Cart
    • Midday meal (a.k.a. Lunch)
    • Snacks
    • Ambiance and more from local DJ Trax Mathews
    • Evening meal (a.k.a. Dinner and Drinks at the after party)
    • Live music from special musical guest, The Superbytes
  • Brought to you by NebraskaJS (Omaha) and organized by the NEJS CONF Team.
